AI has a wide range of applications that impact everyday 
life and industries globally. In healthcare, AI assists in 
medical imaging, diagnosis, and drug discovery.
In finance, it is used for fraud detection, algorithmic trading,
and risk assessment. Autonomous vehicles rely on AI for perception, 
navigation, and decision-making. AI-powered recommendation systems enhance
user experiences on platforms like Netflix, Amazon, and YouTube. Additionally, 
AI is used in robotics, natural language interfaces, smart cities, and climate modeling. By automating tasks, improving accuracy, and providing insights from data,
AI continues to revolutionize modern society.
